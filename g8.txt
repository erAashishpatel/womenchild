                    <p class="lead">
This scheme provides integrated services to women without support from their families such as widows living at Vrindavan and Kashi;</br> prisoners released from jail; survivors of natural calamities; women/girls rescued from brothels and other places; victims of sexual crimes, etc.</br> The scheme includes such services as food, clothing, shelters, health care, counselling and legal aid and rehabilitation through education awareness, skill formation and behavioural training.
