paanchavaan maheena ungalee aur pair kee angulee naakhoon gathit shor ka javaab deta hai baal aur bhauhen badh rahee hain aandolan tejee se joradaar ho jaate hain bhroon lagabhag 25 se 30 semee (10to12inchhaislong) hai, janm kee aadha lambaee aur 220 se 450 graam vajan (8 paund se 1 paund) loha svasth rakt ke lie aayaran mahatvapoorn hai. aapake bachche ke mastishk ko theek se vikasit karane ke lie bhee jarooree hai.aapako paryaapt lauh praapt karane kee zaroorat hai taaki aapaka bachcha theek se badh sake aur lauh kee achchhee dukaan bana sake janm ke baad. paryaapt lohe ke bina baabaon mein adhik beemaariyaan aur samasyaen seekh sakatee hain. apane lohe ko lene ke lie, lohe mein samrddh khaady padaarth jaise laal maans, ande aur murgee; poore khaate hain anaaj aur samrddh rotee aur anaaj; pakaaya ya naiddriaid sem kar sakate hain; aur matar aur masoor. ise khatm mat karo! yadi aap antaraal vitaamin-khanij poorak le rahe hain jisamen kantenar hai,aapako apane doktar, narsar dvaara anushansit atirikt lauh poorak kee aavashyakata nahin hai aahaar visheshagy.
